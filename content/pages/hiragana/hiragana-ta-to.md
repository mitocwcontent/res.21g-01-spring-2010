たかい (takai) expensive

**Printed form:** ち

**Reading:** ti/chi

**Usage Examples:**  
つち (tuti/tsuchi) soil  
ちち (titi/chichi) father

**Printed form:** つ

**Reading:** tu/tsu

**Usage Examples:**  
きつつき (kitutuki/titsutsuki) woodpecker  
つかう (tukau/tsukau) to use

て (te) hand
